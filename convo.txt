# DriveMate voice demo — EV vs ICE
# Driver has two vehicles in the app (Profile → Garage): VinFast VF 8 (EV) and Volvo XC60 (diesel).
# The AI asks which car is in use so running-cost talk stays in kWh/charging vs fuel litres.

Driver: Hi, I’m about to head to the People’s Committee office. What’s the best route today?

AI: Quick check—which car are you taking today? Your electric VinFast VF 8, or your Volvo XC60?

Driver: The VinFast VF 8—I’m going electric today.

AI: Got it. I’ll tune tips for battery use and charging—not petrol—unless you switch vehicles in the app.

AI: I found 3 options:

Best Value Route — 35 mins, 2 toll stations, Toll 30k each.

AI: I recommend Best Value because it saves about 20k in running cost with only a few extra minutes.

AI: There are also Fastest and Cheapest lanes if you want to hear them—energy use looks a bit different on each.

Driver: Just start the best route.

AI: Starting Best Value Route now.

AI: There is a Highlands Coffee on the way. Would you like to grab one as usual? I can remind you when you’re nearby.

Driver: Yes, remind me when I’m close.

AI: Sure. I’ll remind you when you’re near the coffee shop.

Driver: Any traffic I should know about?

AI: Forecast for your inbound leg: the highway stretch is expected to jam from about 7:50 to 8:30 AM—going through the middle often adds around 15 minutes.

AI: Try to leave before about 8:10 AM to miss the worst queue—you can usually save roughly 12 minutes compared with leaving mid-peak.

Driver: What should I expect for energy cost on the VF 8 for this commute?

AI: Blended home and DC fast pricing is up about 120 VND per kilowatt-hour versus your average last month.

AI: For this office run you are looking at roughly 42 to 48k in electricity based on your current charging mix.

AI: The Best Value lane avoids the longest high-speed pulls in this heat, which trims a few percent off kWh per kilometer.

AI: Also, Nguyen Hue Smart Parking near your destination still has EV bays available this morning.

Driver: Reserve one parking spot for me.

AI: Reserved. You now have one EV parking slot from 8:30 to 10:30 AM, around a 6-minute walk to the office.

Driver: Do I need to top up my wallet?

AI: Your wallet balance is 120k.

AI: Estimated toll spending for the next few trips is 300k.

AI: I recommend topping up 200k.

Driver: How many reward points do I have?

AI: You currently have 1,250 points.

AI: You can redeem a coffee voucher or parking discount today.

Driver: How did I do this week?

AI: This week you completed 12 trips.

AI: You saved 120k using smart routes.

AI: You also reduced travel time by 45 minutes.

AI: Great job!

Driver: Nice.

AI: Navigation is active. Have a safe trip!
