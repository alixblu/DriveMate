DRIVEMATE AI – FULL DEMO BUILD SPEC (HACKATHON MVP)

==================================================

PRODUCT SUMMARY
==================================================

DriveMate AI is an AI mobility assistant integrated into VETC that increases daily engagement by helping drivers save time, reduce travel cost, and build smarter habits.

Instead of opening the app only to pay tolls, users open it daily for personalized mobility insights.

Core business KPI:

Increase DAU
Improve retention
Increase wallet usage
Create upsell / partner reward opportunities
